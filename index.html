Personality Development

A short guide to personality development


Personality development is the process of changing,enchancing or developing a person's personality traits,behavious and thought patterns. A person's personality plays an important part in the first impression set by themselves.When we meet people it's often their personailty which grabs our attention.

"Personality is a blend of behavioral and thought patterns that are relatively stable over time, characterizing an individual's traits and attitudes",says Ludovica Colella a CBT therapist and author of "The Feel Good Journal."

Importance of personality development:


  • Self-awareness

    • You can learn more about yourself such as your strengths, your weaknesses,yours values or morals.

  • Self-confidece

    • It helps to build confidence by making us set and achieve goals, overcome obstacles and leatn new skills

  • Fullfilment

    • You can feel a sense of fullfilment as personality development helps you to pursue your passion, using your strenghts, and living in line with your values

  • Long-term Growth

    • It helps in expanding your knowledge and make you learn at every step of your life thus expanding your knowledge

  • Professional success

    • You can lead a team, train them better and help your juniors grow more.
